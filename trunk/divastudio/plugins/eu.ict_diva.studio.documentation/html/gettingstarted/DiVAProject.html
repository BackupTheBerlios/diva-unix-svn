DYNAMIC VARIABILITY IN COMPLEX, ADAPTIVE SYSTEMS

DiVA in a Nutshell

DiVA will provide a new tool-supported methodology with an integrated framework for managing dynamic variability in adaptive systems. This goal will be addressed by combining aspect-oriented and model-driven techniques in an innovative way.

Partners

DiVA mobilises leading players within the domains of model-driven engineering, aspect-oriented analysis and modelling, variability modelling and partners that provide advanced end user systems and applications where the demand for adaptation is a major concern.
